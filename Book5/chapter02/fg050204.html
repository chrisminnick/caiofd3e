Step-by-Step Recipe: Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Gather and measure all dry ingredients:
    1. 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
    3. 1 teaspoon salt
  2. Prepare wet ingredients in a large mixing bowl:
    1. 1 cup (2 sticks) softened butter
    2. 3/4 cup granulated sugar
    3. 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
    4. 2 large eggs
    5. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  3. Combine dry and wet ingredients:
    1. Gradually beat flour mixture into butter mixture
    2. Stir in 2 cups chocolate chips
    3. Mix until well combined
  4. Bake the cookies:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
    2. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets
    3. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown
    4. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire rack
